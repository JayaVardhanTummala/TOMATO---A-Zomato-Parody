Vaishali Vendors

A true heirloom treasure, the Vaishali tomato is the heart of authentic Indian cooking. Its distinct, slightly flattened shape holds a perfect harmony of sweet and tangy notes, providing a rich, complex base that makes curries and gravies truly unforgettable. Choose Vaishali when you want a deep, traditional flavour that speaks of home.

Rupali Vendors

The quintessential curry tomato, Rupali is celebrated for its classic round shape, firm texture, and consistently deep flavour. It holds its shape beautifully when cooked, adding a satisfying body to any dish. Reliable, versatile, and always delicious, Rupali is the workhorse of the Indian kitchen, perfect for everything from a simple dal tadka to a complex masala.

Pusa Ruby Vendors

As radiant as its name suggests, the Pusa Ruby is a small-to-medium tomato bursting with a bright, zesty flavour. Its vibrant red colour and tangy profile are perfect for cutting through richness, making it an ideal choice for chutneys, soups, and sauces. When your recipe needs a lively, fresh kick, the Pusa Ruby delivers every time.
